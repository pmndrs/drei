Abstraction of THREE's LOD (Level of Detail).
Renders different children based on camera distance.

### Basic usage

```jsx
<Detailed distances={[0, 50, 100]}>
  <HighDetailMesh />
  <MediumDetailMesh />
  <LowDetailMesh />
</Detailed>
```
